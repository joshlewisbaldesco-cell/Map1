About the LNU Campus

Leyte Normal University

Leyte Normal University (LNU) is a premier public institution committed to excellence in teacher education, arts, and sciences in the Philippines.

Leyte Normal University is a university in the province of Leyte, Philippines. It is mandated to provide higher professional and special instruction for special purposes and to promote research and extension services, advanced studies, and progressive leadership in education and other related fields.
